How Drone Duo data is parsed:
• COMPLETED match: both teams competed.
• BYE match: the team listed competed (received a bye).
• FORFEITED match: only the team with 1 point competed; the 0-point team did not.
• DOUBLE FORFEITED match: neither team competed.
• Total teams = all unique teams found anywhere in the data.
• Burke Central grouped under Kenmare. Schools sorted A–Z.

#SchoolCompeted / TotalProgressStatus
How Drone Combat data is parsed:
• Paste the full page including both the Group section and the Rankings section.
• Total teams = all teams found in the Rankings section (all registered this week).
• Competed = teams in the Group section with at least 1 win or 1 loss (W+L > 0). Teams with 0W-0L have not played yet (bye/queued).
• Teams are not double-counted between the Group and Rankings sections. • Total teams = all unique teams found in the Rankings section (not just those in matches).
• COMPLETED match: both teams competed.
• BYE match: the listed team competed.
• FORFEITED: only the 1-point team competed.
• DOUBLE FORFEITED: neither competed.
• Teams in rankings but never in any match = did not compete (never queued).
• Burke Central grouped under Kenmare. Schools sorted A–Z.
